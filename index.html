About Us

Platform Where Customer Can Easily Connect With Event Organising Vendors

Welcome to Planora, your all-in-one platform for seamless event planning! At Planora, we believe that every event, big or small, deserves to be perfectly organized and stress-free. Whether you're planning a wedding, birthday party, or corporate event, Planora helps you streamline the process and turn your vision into reality with ease

Our Services

At Planora, we offer a comprehensive suite of services to make your event planning experience smooth and hassle-free. Whether you're organizing a wedding, corporate event, or birthday celebration, our platform is designed to bring your event vision to life. Explore the services we provide:

Vendor Connections

Easily browse and connect with a wide range of vendors, from caterers and decorators to photographers and DJs. Our curated list ensures you only find the best in the business, tailored to meet your event needs.

Customizable Event Packages

Choose from a variety of event packages or build your own! Whether it's a small gathering or a large-scale celebration, Planora offers flexible options to suit every need and budget.

Service Management for Vendors

For vendors, we offer a dedicated space to showcase services, manage bookings, and communicate directly with clients. It’s a powerful tool for expanding your business and reaching the right audience.
